# Hi there, we are BMProjects (BMP) 👋

We are an international independent game development and modding studio. Our mission is to transform the gaming experience, build massive immersive worlds, and set new quality standards in the industry.

By leveraging a **"Living Service"** model, we continuously evolve our projects based on analytics and community feedback, delivering rapid updates and maintaining high operational standards.
